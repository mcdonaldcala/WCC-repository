Sylvia Earle

"I wish you would use all means at your disposal films! expeditions! the web! more! to ignite public support for a global network of marine protected areas, hope spots large enough to save and restore the ocean, the blue heart of the planet.""- Sylvia Earle

Sylvia Earle is a renouned marinebiologist. She was a frontrunner in US diving and marine science and continues to work for the protection all oceans. She has always been a hero of mine.

Why she is awesome aka interesting facts

    First female chief scientist at NOAA
    In 1979, she set the women's diving depth record of 381 meters off the coast of Oahu
    She is currently a National geographic 'explorer in residence'
